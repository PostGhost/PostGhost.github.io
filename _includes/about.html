About Us

The Post Ghost Production Company, established in 2010, has a wide array of production and mastering expertise. We pride ourselves in offering 'The Whole Package' to our clients. With PGPC you'll gain access to an expert-level engineering team utilising the best of hard and soft technologies to get your mix sounding great.

Mission Statement

The Post Ghost technique is to enhance the artist/band as they are and wish to be, smoothly, subtly, and professionally. I’m here to give you the best, loudest, clearest, most dynamic masters and leave you satisfied every time.

Finished Product Summary

I am happy to send free 30 second samples of your masters so that you can hear what you’re getting before you pay for it. The finished product I send you back will be rich, lively, and commercially acceptable. It’s a great idea for radio play, live shows, and to make your album or EP sound great. I’ll deliver physical discs burnt with your finished album on it, as well as e-mailing you bounced .wavs for back-up. Track names will be printed and included on the disc and bounces.
